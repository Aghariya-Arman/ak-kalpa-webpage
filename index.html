    Working with an Interdependent Ecosystem

    Self-sustaining Farms

    We practice closed-loop soil management systems. Our farms do not require external elements to sustain.

    The cow dung is used to produce methane gas, which is then used to generate electricity for day to day activities at the farm. Slurry from the biogas goes back to the soil as manure.

    Happy home

    Our farms provide not just shelters, but a stress-free home for our cows. We make sure our cows have access to clean water and organic fodder as and when they need.  With an open and dedicated paddock area, our cows are healthy, happy and free.

    Explore More >>

    One big happy Family

    We handhold our farmers at every step of the farming operations ensuring economic viability and technical support. With about 18-24 months training, we groom our farmers on the organic farming practices including maintaining soil without chemicals, closed loop soil management and raising cows without the use of antibiotics or growth hormones.

    Explore More >>

    The food of nature

    With no antibiotics and preservatives, we bring your food from the farms, just like you wish. We perform a series of tests at every turn, to make sure that every Akshayakalpa product is organic and pure, before it reaches the doors of the customers.
